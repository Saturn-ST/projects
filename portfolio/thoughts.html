THOUGHTS

Very First Post

     My very first post on my very first sight that I built myself from scratch! Yay!

     I've messed around with web dev on and off for so many years but would get this idea in my head that if I wasn't a professional within 3 months of starting to learn that I was behind somehow. Those ideas lead to researching coding bootcamps and other learning resources, and eventually analysis paralysis until I lost interest and moved on to something else. I'm not letting that happen again. Instead of setting goals with extrinsic rewards. My goal is to follow my curiosity and learn whatever looks fun and interesting.

     I'm loving CSS so far. Even in the last few years since I started messing around things have moved forward so much in it's capabilities that I'm thrilled to be getting back into it.

     I hope this is the beginning of many more sites and posts to come.

     I hope you enjoy my sight. It's a compilation of designs from around the web and things that look interesting to me today.

     I'm going to be creating a post about my inspirations. Stay tuned!
