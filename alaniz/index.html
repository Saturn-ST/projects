Relationship Counseling for Individuals and Couples

Relationships bring immense joy to our lives and can also cause us some of our greatest pain. Sometimes great relationships can get stuck in negative patterns which over time cause feelings of anger, frustration or disconnection. If you are feeling helpless and ready to throw the towel in....don't! There still may be hope! I would like to work with you and your loved ones in communicating better, and sharing your true self with each other. But couples counseling shouldn't only be a "last case resort." It is also something that can take good relationships to great! We live in such a fast paced society that its important to nurture the relationships that mean the most. Reach out and schedule a session.

Individual Therapy & Psychotherapy

As your therapist, I want to first learn your story then help you live your best story. Finding the right therapist is all about connection and results. Please reach out by phone and introduce yourself. You can get a feel for my personality and see if we are a good match. I provide a safe place for clients to take a non judgmental view of their life in order to gain insight, and then provide the support needed to make change. I tailor my approach to your individual needs and am committed to your personal growth. Once you become a client of mine, I'm on your team! Whatever is holding you back from being your best self is what I want to help you conquer. The human potential is amazing! I believe in you and committed to helping you become your best self.

Conflict And Transition

Sometimes life takes us on twists and turns. Therapy can be essential in times of transition because stress can become overwhelming. This keeps us from making our best choices. I want to support you along this journey. We can't control our circumstances, but we can control how we react, think, and respond. I want to you help you regain control of situations which make you feel overwhelmed. Reach out and take the next step.

Frequently Asked Questions

Clients who work with Gabriel typically meet with him either 1x per week or every 2 weeks. Gabriel has noticed clients who work with him see the most progress with consistent sessions over a period of time.

Gabriel Does not work directly with insurance companies. Therapy is out of pocket in the form of cash, check, credit card, or HSA Account.

If you have a PPO you can submit for reimbursement directly through the insurance company and depending upon diagnosis you may get reimbursed a percentage.

Gabriel can provide a superbill for this purpose.

Gabriel does not provide any form of sliding scale.
